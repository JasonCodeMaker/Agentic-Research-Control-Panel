# Guide: Conclusion

## Role
Close at the scope the evidence supports. Summarize, bound, and point forward once.

## Structure
1. Contribution summary in 2–3 sentences.
2. Honest limitations: what the method cannot handle, what assumptions it relies on.
3. One specific future direction (not a wishlist).

## Rules
- Stay inside the scope the results support — do not broaden claims in the conclusion.
- Limitations are concrete, not ritual hedging.

## Do not
- "Taken together, these findings suggest…" / "future research should explore…" (open-ended filler).
- Introduce a new claim or result that did not appear earlier.

## Paragraph roles to expect
synthesis (summary) → limitation → forward pointer.
