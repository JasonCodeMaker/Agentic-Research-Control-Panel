# Attribution & Licenses

The `paper-writing` component adapts rules, templates, and workflow shape from three
open-source skills. None of their prose is copied into user manuscripts; only the
**writing methodology** (stages, checklists, section templates, style heuristics) is
adapted here. All three are MIT-licensed.

| Layer | Source project | Author / Copyright | License | What was adapted |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| L1 Workflow kernel | `paper-writing-skill` (SNL-UCSB) | Arpit Gupta, 2026 | MIT | Five-stage lifecycle, introduction-twice rule, section order, style-audit gate, figure data/non-data split. Systems/networking defaults moved into `profiles/systems_networking.md`. |
| L2 Project/venue adapter | `journal-adapt-writing-skill` | 2026 | MIT | Style-card → style-profile → dynamic adapter flow, hard-preserve rules, conflict table, human gate, corpus-conversion-before-extraction discipline. |
| L3 Global guide bank | `Research-Paper-Writing-Skills` | Master-cai, 2026 | MIT | Per-section guides (abstract/intro/related-work/method/experiments/conclusion), paragraph-flow / reverse-outline check, reviewer self-check, load-only-current-section rule. |

Original sources are kept read-only under `.source/paper-writing/` for provenance.
Full license texts ship with each source project's `LICENSE` file.
