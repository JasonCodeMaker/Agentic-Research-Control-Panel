# Profile: ML Conference

A lightweight profile for top ML/CV/NLP conferences. Inherits everything from `ml_dl_general.md` and
adds conference-submission conventions. Use for NeurIPS, ICLR, ICML, ACL, EMNLP, NAACL, CVPR, ICCV,
ECCV, AAAI.

## Submission conventions

- Hard page limit; references and appendix usually excluded — confirm per call.
- Double-blind by default: no author/institution leaks, no non-anonymous links, careful self-citation.
- Colon-style section subtitles are common ("Method: Event-Centric Encoding").
- Reproducibility / responsible-research checklist where the venue requires it.

## Section emphasis

- Framing as a **methodological advance**, not operational impact.
- Related work is integrated or standalone — pick one and stay consistent.
- A teaser/pipeline figure on the first page strengthens first impression (especially CVPR/ICCV/ECCV).

## Compression

- Compress to fit; do **not** pad to fill the page limit. A short, dense paper beats a padded one.

Defers to `ml_dl_general.md` for contribution framing, evidence discipline, hedging, and voice. A
target-venue corpus adapter (P2) overrides these defaults when present.
